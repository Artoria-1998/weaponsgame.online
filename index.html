Kingdom Battle 3D

Command your stickman army, conquer enemy castles, and claim victory in this epic 3D strategy battle!

Better on Desktop

This game is best experienced on a desktop computer with a keyboard and mouse.

Game Overview

Welcome to Kingdom Battle 3D, a thrilling real-time strategy game that puts you in command of a stickman army. Your mission is to deploy your forces, manage your resources, and lead a calculated assault to capture the enemy's castle. With stunning 3D graphics and intuitive gameplay, every battle is a unique test of your strategic mind.

Key Features

  • Strategic Gameplay: Think on your feet! Manage your mana, spawn different types of units, and outsmart your opponent.
  • Engaging 3D Battles: Watch the battlefield come to life with vibrant 3D graphics and dynamic stickman animations.
  • Multiple Levels: Face increasingly challenging opponents and conquer a variety of maps on your path to glory.

How to Play

  1. Deploy Units: Click on the unit cards at the bottom of the screen to send your soldiers into battle.
  2. Manage Mana: Keep an eye on your mana bar. It refills over time and is required to spawn new units.
  3. Capture the Castle: Your objective is to overwhelm the enemy's defenses and destroy their main castle.
